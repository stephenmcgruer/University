Overview

This page contains a list of courses that I took during my time at university, for which I have coursework-related files. I hope that the work stored in these repositories might be useful to someone, either as a learning tool or simply for interest. However, I would caution the reader against two things. Firstly, although this work is my own, remember that most pieces of coursework are completed under tight time constraints, with an eye only on making it 'work' for those short few minutes it is being evaluated. As such, little of this work was written with maintainability in mind[^1], and so is definitely not production quality in any sense. Secondly, if you are still a student (particularly at Edinburgh University), it would be unwise to try and submit this work as your own; aside from the potential for your coursework to be subtly (or not so subtly) different from mine, the fact that all of these repositories are publicly accessible means it's very easy for plagiarism software to check them! Of course, since this is released under the WTFPL license (see below) I can't instruct you on what to do or no to do with it, only counsel...

[^1] And often has way too many comments, as one can never be sure if a marker will actually understand the code...
